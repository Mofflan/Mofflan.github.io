FABRIK (Procedual Animation) | WIP

FABRIK

Fabrik, short for Forward and Backward Reaching Inverse Kinematics, is a game-changing technique in animation and robotics. It's a mathematical algorithm that allows precise control of articulated structures, enabling lifelike movements in digital characters. This is my journey on FABRIK.

The Struggle Begins

at the beginning my plan was to make a spider that got procedual animation in unreal engine but I got so many problems with rigging and unreal. My first plan was just to make one leg for the spider and try to make a IK but I couldn't even export the leg.

When i exported it to unreal i couldn't find my skeleton and I was so confusing, i tried so many times to export as fbx to unreal but the skeleton wouldn't show up so after many tries i switched to unity.

In unity for some reason the skeleton show up and I didn't understand why but it at least worked. that was my export/import problem but after i switched to unity and tried to move my leg with FABRIK the mesh just started to stretch, it looks so bad.
