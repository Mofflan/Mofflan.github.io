Enemy System

In this project, the enemies are turret-like units that fire arrows behaving like hitscan projectiles. To simulate pressure without unpredictable unfair spikes, we use an invisible pursuing orb that constantly chases the player. When it reaches the player, damage is applied and the orb resets to its starting slot in a path/list. This works like a soft timer that rewards momentum: the orb's speed and tether distance scale dynamically with the player's current velocity.

The enemy accuracy system is intentionally tuned for immersion: as the player moves faster, aim cone error widens so shots visually "miss" more often, reinforcing a feeling of narrowly dodging rather than pure RNG. This keeps combat readable while supporting the parkour focus.
