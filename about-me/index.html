Hello! My name is Mathias Perez Bustos, and I'm a passionate programmer who loves creating games with a team of friends. While I primarily focus on front-end development, I occasionally dabble in back-end tasks as well. One of my favorite projects was "Jakten på Pannkakstårta," where I had the opportunity to work on the dialogue system and romance aspects of the game.

My love for gaming started at a very young age. One of my earliest memories is playing a Spider-Man game while wearing gloves that resembled the superhero's iconic suit. Since then, gaming has been big part of my life. During my free time, you'll often find me browsing on my computer or engaging in gaming nights with friends, which have become a cherished evening ritual.

Recently, I've also discovered a new passion: playing Magic: The Gathering with my friends. I have been borrowing my friends dinosaour deck.

As a programmer, I'm constantly seeking opportunities to learn and grow. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can contribute my skills and ideas to create immersive and engaging gaming experiences. If you're looking for a dedicated and enthusiastic team member who is committed to bringing virtual worlds to life, I'd love to connect with you!
