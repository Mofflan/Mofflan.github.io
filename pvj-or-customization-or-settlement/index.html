Internship at ION Game Design
I completed an 8-month internship at ION Game Design, where I was promoted to Team Lead, managing a cross-disciplinary team of ~10 members, including programmers, 2D/3D artists, and a technical artist.

During my time there, I worked on many different features and systems, including:

  • Customization systems for player assets

  • Save system to persist game progress

  • Tooling for quick and efficient access to JSON files

  • Unlockable content features

  • Mini-games and various gameplay mechanics

These are just a few examples—throughout the internship, I contributed to many other areas of development as well.
